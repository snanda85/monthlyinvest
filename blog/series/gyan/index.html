Basics of Investing

How to start thinking about investments

The habit of investment cannot be the result of an impulsive decision, but rather a gradual change in the mindset. Although investing a lumpsum amount is not bad, it is actually the regular savings that helps in accumulating wealth over time. Slowly and steadily, regular investing becomes a habit that can reward you in future. It provides the discipline to allocate money for saving before you have the temptation to spend it.
